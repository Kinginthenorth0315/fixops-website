HubSpot doesn't tell you when things go wrong. No alerts for silent failures. No warning before you hit your billing tier. No flag when a dashboard starts pulling corrupt data. That's what FixOps is for.

Workflows failing silently

HubSpot marks a workflow "Active" even when actions are failing. Leads disappear. Reps don't get tasks. Nobody finds out until someone complains. This is the #1 revenue leak we find every audit.

FAQs

Good Questions.
Honest Answers.

Via read-only OAuth, FixOps reads: contacts, companies, deals, tickets, notes, tasks, calls, emails, meetings, properties, property groups, workflows (structure, actions, enrollment counts, error logs), pipelines, lists, forms, email campaign stats, integrations, users, and teams. We cannot read private note content or access data your HubSpot plan doesn't expose via the API. You can revoke our access from your HubSpot portal at any time.
Yes — for specific, well-defined fixes: archiving dead workflows, standardizing field values (country, phone format, job title), reassigning unowned contacts, and removing junk records. Every auto-fix shows you a full preview of what will change before anything is touched. Every change has a 30-day rollback window. We never auto-fix workflow logic, delete properties, modify pipelines, or make structural changes — those always go through our team with explicit approval from you.
RevSignal pulls from HubSpot's Deals, Contacts, Engagements, and Properties APIs to calculate: MRR trends (from closed-won deal amounts and close dates), pipeline velocity (time per stage), rep performance (activity and deal data), and forecast accuracy. Churn signals use email engagement and ticket data. Multi-touch attribution requires Marketing Hub Professional or Enterprise — we're transparent about this in the platform. You'll never see a metric we can't calculate from your actual data.
FixOps works with all HubSpot plans — Free, Starter, Professional, and Enterprise. The audit depth scales with your plan since some API endpoints are only available on Professional and Enterprise. We clearly show in the platform what's available at your tier. The core audit — data integrity, workflow health, pipeline gaps, configuration, and reporting quality — works on all plans.
Every issue card in the platform has a "Fix It For Me" button. Clicking it sends a pre-filled request to our team with the full context — what the issue is, what it's costing you, and what needs to happen. We review it, scope the work, and send a flat-rate quote within 4 hours. You approve the quote, we do the work. No open-ended billing, no surprise charges. Larger projects (builds, migrations, RevOps strategy) start with a discovery call to scope properly.
FixOps works best for growth-stage and scaling businesses — SaaS, professional services, agencies, manufacturing, e-commerce — that are already using HubSpot and want it to work harder. If you have more than 2,000 contacts, any active workflows, or a sales team using the CRM daily, you'll find things in your audit that surprise you. We've yet to run an audit that came back clean.
