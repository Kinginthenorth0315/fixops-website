FAQs

Good Questions. Honest Answers.

What can FixOps actually read from my HubSpot portal? +
Via read-only OAuth, FixOps reads: contacts, companies, deals, tickets, tasks, calls, emails, meetings, workflows (structure, triggers, actions, enrollment counts, error logs, ownership, age), pipelines, properties, lists, forms, email campaigns (including sender domain, email type, A/B test status), sequences, connected integrations and their sync health, import history and error rates, API rate limit usage, users, teams, and security activity logs. We cannot read private note content or any data your HubSpot plan doesn't expose via the API. You can revoke our access from your HubSpot connected apps settings at any time.
How is FixOps different from HubSpot's native Data Hub? +
HubSpot Data Hub (formerly Operations Hub) is an Enterprise-only tool focused on data sync and automation — it doesn't audit your portal health, flag workflow errors, calculate dollar waste, or give you rep intelligence. FixOps works across all HubSpot tiers and specifically finds problems Data Hub doesn't look for: broken workflow logic, billing tier inflation, deliverability risks, dark funnel attribution, and the 200+ configuration checks that require cross-referencing multiple data sources.
There are other audit tools on the HubSpot marketplace. What makes this different? +
Most audit tools on the marketplace do one thing: generate a PDF snapshot of your portal at a single point in time. You get a report, maybe a score, and a list of recommendations. Then you're on your own to fix them — and on your own to figure out if new problems appeared next week.

FixOps is built around a different premise: a single audit is a starting point, not the product. The real value is continuous. Every week your portal is rescanned automatically. Every finding has a dollar amount tied to your exact HubSpot pricing, not a generic benchmark. Every issue has a Fix It For Me option where our team delivers a flat-rate proposal from your actual data within 24 hours. And an AI layer lets you query your live portal data in plain English instead of waiting for the next report.

The short version: other tools diagnose. FixOps diagnoses, monitors every week, and executes the fix — all under one platform without an agency retainer.
Why would I use FixOps instead of just hiring a HubSpot agency? +
A typical agency charges $150–$250/hr and needs 2–4 weeks of discovery calls, intake forms, and scoping meetings before they even send a proposal — and the final bill is almost always higher than quoted. FixOps scopes every engagement directly from your audit data. We already know what's wrong before the first call. Proposals go out within 24 hours at a flat rate you approve before anything starts. Most single-issue fixes cost 50–70% less than agency rates. For ongoing monitoring, $199–$399/mo replaces what agencies charge $2,000–$5,000/mo to do manually.
How does the Fix It For Me service actually work? +
Run a free audit. When you see an issue, click "Fix It For Me." We review the specific finding from your portal data and send a flat-rate proposal within 24 hours. The proposal includes exactly what we'll do, what we won't touch, and the price. You approve and we schedule. For larger engagements — a full portal cleanup, a hub implementation, or an integration — book a 30-minute call and we'll scope from your full audit report. No discovery process, no retainer required.
Is the revenue waste estimate accurate, or is it inflated to impress me? +
We split the estimate into two buckets. Confirmed Waste uses exact HubSpot pricing — ghost seats at $90/seat/month (published HubSpot pricing), duplicate contacts at $0.18/contact/month (mid-point of the 5k-10k Marketing Hub tier math). These are exact. At-Risk Revenue uses conservative industry benchmarks applied to your actual deal data — stalled pipeline at 2%/month close rate decay (HubSpot deal velocity research). We show you the math on every card. If you have zero stalled deals with dollar amounts, we show $0 — not a fake estimate.
What HubSpot tiers and plan sizes do you support? +
FixOps works across all HubSpot tiers — Free, Starter, Professional, and Enterprise. Portals with 1,000 contacts to 500,000+ contacts all run fine. Some checks (Sequences, Playbooks, Breeze AI readiness) are specific to Professional and Enterprise tiers — if you're on a lower tier, those checks are skipped gracefully. The audit adapts to what's available in your specific portal.
Can I use FixOps to audit client portals as an agency? +
Yes. Sentinel includes white-label client sharing — generate a shareable URL and branded PDF reports with your agency name on them, for client deliverables. Command is built specifically for agencies managing multiple portals: full white-label portal where clients log in at your domain with your branding, plus each client portal gets its own audit, dashboard, and monitoring. We also do partner engagements where you run FixOps audits for your clients and our team handles the Fix It For Me work — reach out to discuss.
