✓ Best Practice Guide
1
Go to Contacts → Actions → Manage Duplicates. Review high-confidence matches first — these are safe to merge immediately.
2
In your Zapier integration, add a "Find Contact" step before "Create Contact." If found, update; if not, create. Eliminates 412 duplicates at the source.
3
Add a hidden "Form Source" field to all HubSpot forms so every contact has a trackable origin. Prevents future duplicates from forms.
4
Schedule a monthly dedup review. FixOps can automate this so duplicates never accumulate to 847 again.

Enable FixOps Sentinel to alert you within minutes of any workflow error — before leads are lost.
