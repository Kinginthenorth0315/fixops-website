How White-Label Audits Work
1
Set your branding
Upload your logo, set your brand colors, and add a footer message. This branding appears on all client audit results pages and reports.
2
Create a client audit link
Enter your client's name, email, and company. We generate a unique audit link — it uses 1 credit from your monthly allowance.
3
Send the link to your client
Your client clicks the link, connects their HubSpot via OAuth, and the 210-point audit runs automatically. Takes about 15 minutes. No setup required on their end.
4
Client sees YOUR brand
The results page, full reporting dashboard, and PDF export all show your agency name, logo, and colors. FixOps is invisible — your client thinks this is your proprietary tool.
5
You get notified & can view all results
You get an email when the audit completes with score and key findings. All client audits appear in this dashboard with branded view links you can share directly.
